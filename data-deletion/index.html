How to Delete Your Data

If you would like to request the deletion of your user data from the Sixaloa Industries Meta Management App, please send an email to:

awt@sixaloa.com

In your email, please include:

• Your name
• The email address associated with your account
• A request to delete your data

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.
